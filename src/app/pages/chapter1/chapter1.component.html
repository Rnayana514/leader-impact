* Fulfilment of prophecies made hundreds of years before the birth of Christ is another evidence of the veracity of the claims of Christ. It is not possible these events happened by mere coincidence -- Peter W. Stoner and Robert C. Newman wrote a book entitled Science Speaks. The book was based on the science of probability and vouched for by the American Scientific Affiliation. It set out the odds of any one man in all of history fulfilling even only eight of the 60 major prophecies (and 270 ramifications) fulfilled by the life of Christ. The probability that Jesus of Nazareth could have fulfilled even eight such prophecies would be only 1 in 1e+17 . This would be like covering an area twice the size of Rajasthan - the largest state in India - with one rupee coins two feet deep, stirring in one coin marked with an X and then asking a blindfolded man to be randomly dropped into the pile and randomly select the marked coin out of 1017coins on his very first attempt.

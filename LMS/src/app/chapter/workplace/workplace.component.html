It is said that everything rises or falls on leadership.

Whether you are a student, housewife, labourer or professional ... whether you are in business or in private or public service ... we believe God has made each person for a particular purpose and is calling him or her to Himself and uniquely preparing them to honour Him by serving through their unique vocation. Your calling is a high calling! Your vocation IS ministry!!

It is within this vocational context that we are also called first to be and then to make disciples of Jesus Christ. Guided by His Word, aligned through prayer and empowered by His Spirit, we are to lead out and excel in our vocation, reflect the characteristics of His Kingdom, and minister to society through service, prayer, proclamation of the Good News of the Kingdom and discipleship.

To this end we seek to build Kingdom Leaders in every sphere of influence in society. These Kingdom Leaders should exemplify the following characteristics:

1. They walk with and are sure of their calling.
2. They understand how Scripture applies to their vocation.
3. They have a transformative influence on society as they innovate Spirit-inspired solutions to challenges in their vocation.
4. They make disciple-makers
- Winning people to Christ as a way of life in the context of their vocation
- Building those new believers in their faith
- Equipping and Sending them to in turn win, build and send others to do the same.
5. They have a godly ambition to excel professionally to expand their influence for the sake of the
